One-Pan Lemon Garlic Salmon with Roasted Vegetables

A healthy, flavorful meal ready in 30 minutes!

Prep Time 10 mins
Cook Time 20 mins
Servings 2-3
Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

For the Salmon:

  • 2 salmon fillets (about 150g each)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • Fresh dill or parsley for garnish

For the Vegetables:

  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika

Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Season Salmon: In a small bowl, mix 2 tbsp olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper. Brush this mixture over salmon fillets.
  3. Prepare Vegetables: Toss all vegetables with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  4. Arrange: Spread vegetables on the baking sheet, leaving space for salmon. Place salmon fillets in the center.
  5. Bake: Cook for 15-20 minutes until salmon flakes easily and vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs and extra lemon wedges.

Nutritional Info (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 380 kcal
  • Protein: 28g
  • Carbs: 12g
  • Fats: 25g (healthy omega-3s)
  • Benefits: High in protein, vitamin D, and antioxidants

Chef's Tips

  • For crispier vegetables, broil for the last 2-3 minutes
  • Substitute salmon with trout or cod if preferred
  • Add asparagus or broccoli for extra veggies
  • Serve with quinoa or crusty bread to soak up juices

Crispy Baked Chicken Wings with Garlic-Honey Glaze

Difficulty: Easy

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 45 mins

Serves: 2-3

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken wings (split into drumettes and flats)
  • 1 tbsp baking powder (not soda! – for extra crispiness)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp black pepper

For the Glaze:

  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Prep Wings:
    • Pat wings dry with paper towels (critical for crispiness).
    • In a bowl, toss wings with baking powder, salt, paprika, and pepper.
  2. Bake:
    • Arrange wings on a wire rack over a baking tray (prevents sticking).
    • Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 45 mins, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.
  3. Make Glaze:
    • In a saucepan, sauté garlic until fragrant.
    • Add honey, soy sauce, hot sauce, and butter. Simmer for 2 mins.
  4. Coat & Serve:
    • Toss baked wings in the glaze.
    • Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped herbs.

Nutritional Info (Per Serving)

  • Calories: ~300 kcal
  • Protein: 22g | Carbs: 12g
  • Health Benefits: High protein, no deep-frying, lower glycemic index than fried potatoes

Chef's Notes

For a vegetarian version, replace wings with firm tofu cubes (press dry first) and follow the same method!

Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days. Reheat in oven to maintain crispiness.
